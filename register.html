Registration Information

Registration General Details

• Authors must complete the registration process before uploading the camera-ready version of their paper.

• One registration may cover 1 paper of which the registrant is co-author.

• Every attending author and co-author is required to register separately. Please note that one registration only allows attendance for that one registrant.

• For registration, accepted authors are required to use the same e-mail address that was used for submitting the paper.

• At least one full (i.e., non-student) registration is required to upload a camera ready paper. If all the authors of an accepted paper are students, at least one author must register at a non-student rate.


• REGISTRATION FEE INCLUDES:
◦ Participation in all technical sessions of the conference program;
◦ All meals during the course of the conference;
◦ Conference accessories;
◦ Conference Soft Proceedings;
